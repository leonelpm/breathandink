Breath & Ink
Illustrations of the Inner Path

Drawn by someone who practices the path.

I'm Leonel Palacios (Lochan Das). I'm a scientific illustrator and aerospace engineer who draws orbital mechanics and relativity at my studio HiveHue. This space is something else. Here I share what I study, practice and live in Kriya Yoga, in the lineage of Babaji through Panchanan Bhattacharya, under the guidance of my teacher Nandikesha.

What I do is translate the invisible into clear images. The subtle realm, concepts of the path, the architecture of the practice itself. I draw them with the same visual language I use for physics. Clean geometry, simple forms, a deliberate palette of a few colours, and flat composition inherited from the science posters of the mid twentieth century. I am not trying to teach anyone or gather followers. I draw to understand better what I practice, and I share these drawings in case they serve someone else on their own way.

About / Approach

A practitioner who draws

My day work is scientific illustration grounded in aerospace engineering. The discipline I bring from there is the same one I bring here. Draw only what you have actually studied, and let the structure be true.

So these are not decorative mandalas. They are attempts to map what the teachings describe, honestly and clearly, the way a diagram should. When I do not understand something yet, I do not draw it.

The style

Clean geometry and simple forms, a deliberate palette of a few colours, bold typography, and flat composition drawn from 1930s to 60s posters. The same language as HiveHue, turned toward the inner world.
